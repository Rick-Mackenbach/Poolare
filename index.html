How it works

Three steps to unclaimed savings

01

Connect your travel system

Read-only access to Concur, Cytric, Perk, or others. We pull your booking history, the analysis begins.

02

We identify demand clusters

Our system detects every moment employees were in the same city on overlapping dates, anonymously pooled across companies. These are your untapped group rate opportunities.

03

Hotels compete for your block

We send the demand signal to the hotels in the cluster by either email or AI voice call. They bid against each other. Best rate wins.

The model

The GPO model.
Applied to hotel room nights for the first time.

Group Purchasing Organisations have pooled demand to negotiate lower prices in healthcare, foodservice, and logistics for over 100 years. Nobody had applied it to corporate hotel bookings — until now.
